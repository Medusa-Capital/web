## ANALYSIS PROMPTS FOR LLM

Please analyze this landing page copy and provide:

### 1. Overall Conversion Score (1-100)
Rate the landing page's effectiveness for converting visitors into course enrollees.

### 2. Strengths

### 3. Weaknesses

### 4. Section-by-Section Analysis
For each section, provide:
- Effectiveness score (1-10)
- What's working
- What's not working
- Specific rewrite suggestions

### 5. Headline Analysis
- Do the headlines create urgency?
- Are they clear and benefit-focused?
- Do they address the target audience's pain points?

### 6. CTA Analysis
- Are the CTAs compelling?
- Is there clarity on what happens next?
- Are there enough CTAs throughout the page?

### 7. Social Proof Analysis
- Are the testimonials believable and specific?
- Is the social proof strategically placed?
- Are there missing social proof elements?

### 8. Objection Handling
- Does the FAQ adequately address common objections?
- Are there missing objections that should be addressed?

### 9. Value Proposition Clarity
- Is it clear what the visitor will get?
- Is the unique selling proposition (USP) clear?
- Is pricing/investment mentioned (or strategically omitted)?

### 10. Trust & Credibility
- Are the team credentials compelling?
- Is there enough proof of expertise?
- Are there missing trust elements?

### 11. Emotional Triggers
- Does the copy tap into fears (missing out, losing money)?
- Does it tap into desires (financial freedom, expertise)?
- Is the emotional journey well-constructed?

### 12. Target Audience Alignment
- Does the copy speak directly to "small-to-medium traditional investors"?
- Is the language appropriate for the sophistication level?
- Are there terms that might confuse or alienate the audience?

### 13. Top 5 Priority Improvements
List the 5 changes that would have the biggest impact on conversion.
